Fun Fact

Volcano Fun Facts

Did you know? There are approximately 1,500 potentially active volcanoes around the world, and about 50 of them erupt each year! These eruptions can range from slow lava flows to massive explosive events that can shoot ash high into the atmosphere. Some of the most active volcanoes, like Kīlauea in Hawaii and Mount Etna in Italy, have been erupting for decades or even centuries!
